Gate Keeper - Company Access Control Changes

The Gate Keeper module now enforces company-based access for Controllers and Devices.

1. Controller company ownership

- Each Controller belongs to one company through the company_id field.
- The default company is the current user's active company.
- Users should only work with Controllers that belong to their allowed companies.

2. Device company inheritance

- Each Device belongs to a Controller.
- The Device company is inherited from its Controller through a related company_id field.
- This keeps Device visibility aligned with Controller visibility.

3. Record rules

- A record rule was added for t4.gate_keeper.controller.
- A record rule was added for t4.gate_keeper.device.
- Both rules filter records by:

  company_id child_of company_ids

This means normal users can access Controllers and Devices that belong to companies available to their current Odoo session and their descendant companies.

4. Result

- When a user is working under Company A, they can access Controllers of Company A and Company A's child companies.
- Child companies do not automatically access Controllers from their parent companies.
- Devices follow the same rule because their company comes from their Controller.

5. Company hierarchy view

- A Gate Keeper Companies menu was added.
- The action lists companies in the current allowed company hierarchy.
- The company form shows company information and a Controllers tab.
- Child companies are not shown inside the parent company's notebook.
